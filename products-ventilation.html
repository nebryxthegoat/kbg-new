Ventilation Louvers

Comprehensive range of engineered ventilation louvers for optimal airflow control and weather protection. From heavy-duty industrial systems to architectural solutions—AMCA certified, corrosion-resistant, and designed for critical HVAC applications across commercial and industrial sectors.

PEB Industrial Shed Louvers

Heavy-duty ventilation for Pre-Engineered Buildings and industrial warehouses. Designed to withstand harsh weather while ensuring continuous airflow in manufacturing facilities.

Fixed Blade Louvers

Stationary blade systems providing consistent ventilation with water drainage channels. Ideal for commercial HVAC applications requiring reliable weather protection.

Operable Louvers

Motorized or manual blade control for variable airflow regulation. Perfect for buildings requiring dynamic ventilation management and energy efficiency.

Acoustic Louvers

Sound-attenuating ventilation systems for noise-sensitive environments. Combines airflow efficiency with superior acoustic performance for hospitals and data centers.

Sand Trap Louvers

Specialized blade geometry prevents sand and dust ingress in desert environments. Essential for Middle East projects and industrial sites in arid regions.

Storm Resistant Louvers

High-wind rated systems tested for hurricane and cyclone zones. Engineered for coastal installations requiring maximum weather protection.

Drainable Blade Louvers

Multi-stage water separation with internal drainage gutters. Prevents water penetration in high-rainfall areas while maintaining airflow efficiency.

Penthouse & Rooftop Louvers

Architectural louvers for rooftop mechanical equipment screening. Combines ventilation performance with aesthetic appeal for commercial building facades.

Combination Louvers (Intake/Exhaust)

Dual-purpose systems with separate intake and exhaust sections. Maximizes ventilation efficiency for mechanical rooms and equipment enclosures.

All Louvers AMCA Certified

Every louver system is tested and certified by the Air Movement and Control Association (AMCA) for air performance, water penetration resistance, and structural integrity. Available in custom sizes with powder coating, anodizing, or mill finish options.

Noise Control Systems

Engineered soundproofing solutions for highways, industrial zones, and urban environments. High-performance noise barriers and acoustic systems designed to reduce sound pollution while maintaining durability.

Highway Noise Barriers

Highway Noise Barriers are engineered systems designed to reduce sound pollution caused by vehicular traffic, industrial machinery, or railways. Made of high-strength materials with acoustic insulation layers that absorb and block noise transmission. Available in metal, acrylic, or composite finishes for both functional sound dampening and visual aesthetics.

Key Features

High Noise Reduction
Custom Heights & Modular Lengths
Weatherproof & UV-Resistant
Fast Installation
High Wind Load Resistance
Low Maintenance
Types GI/Aluminum Perforated | Polycarbonate Noise Barrier
Finishes Powder Coated, Paint Finish
Applications Highways, Railways, Industrial Zones, Urban Roads

Acoustic Louvers

Acoustic Louvers are designed to reduce noise from HVAC systems, generators, and industrial equipment while allowing airflow. They combine aerodynamic efficiency with sound attenuation, making them suitable for industrial, commercial, and infrastructure projects.

Key Features

High Noise Reduction
Allows Ventilation
Weather & Corrosion Resistant
Available in GI, Aluminum, SS
Optional Bird/Insect Mesh
Rockwool Insulation

Applications

  • HVAC systems and air handling units
  • Generator rooms and DG set enclosures
  • Industrial equipment ventilation
  • Power plant and turbine systems
  • Commercial building mechanical rooms
Materials GI, Aluminum, Stainless Steel
Finishes Powder Coated, PVDF Coating, Paint Finish

Acoustic Panels & Enclosures

Acoustic panels and enclosures are used for machine soundproofing and noise containment in industrial and commercial spaces. These modular systems help achieve compliance with CPCB noise limits through engineered sound absorption and isolation.

Key Features

CPCB Compliant
Modular Design
Custom Engineering
High Noise Attenuation

Applications

  • DG set enclosures
  • Pump room soundproofing
  • Compressor and blower enclosures
  • Industrial workshop partitions

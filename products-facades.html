Architectural Facade Solutions

Modern, high-performance facade systems combining aesthetics with functionality. From baffle louvers to WPC cladding and perforated panels—engineered for contemporary commercial architecture.

Baffle Louvers / Fins

U-Shape Cladding is a modern and versatile alternative to traditional linear cladding systems. Designed with open vertical or horizontal baffles, it offers a bold and continuous visual effect without the need for fully enclosed paneling.

Profiles U-Shape Cladding | Curve R Cladding

WPC Cladding Panel

WPC (Wood-Plastic Composite) Cladding is a modern facade solution that combines the natural aesthetic of wood with the durability of polymers. These lightweight panels are ideal for exterior applications such as building facades, boundary walls, commercial elevations, and soffits.

Key Features

Corrosion Resistant
Anti-Fading
Weather Proof
Fire Retardant
Termite Proof
Environment Friendly

Applications

  • Building facades (commercial & residential)
  • Boundary walls and compound fencing
  • Parking and podium levels
  • Elevation feature walls
  • Soffit and under-ceiling panels
  • Resort and hospitality exteriors
Panel Types WPC Slatted Panel | WPC Fluted Cladding
Finishes Mahogany, Teak, Walnut, Carbon Black, White Oak

Perforated Cladding

Perforated Cladding Panels are a high-performance architectural solution combining aesthetics with airflow, shading, and acoustic control. Manufactured from lightweight aluminum or GI sheets, these panels feature precision-cut perforations in a variety of shapes—round, slot, square, or custom patterns.

Key Features

Custom Perforation Patterns
Airflow Control
Acoustic Shading
Lightweight Design
Materials Galvanized Iron, Aluminum, Stainless Steel, ACP Sheet
Perforation Shapes Round, Slot, Square, Custom Patterns

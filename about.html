Company Overview

Manufacturer of HVAC, Architectural & Acoustic Ventilation Solutions

KBG Group is an ISO 9001:2015 certified manufacturer and exporter of precision-engineered aluminium profiles, ventilation louvers, acoustic facades, and custom architectural systems. With a focus on innovation, precision, and performance, we deliver high-quality engineered products to clients across India and globally.

Our state-of-the-art manufacturing facility in Nashik is equipped with advanced CNC machinery and comprehensive QA systems to ensure consistency, reliability, and precision in every unit we produce. From design to delivery, we maintain stringent quality controls that meet international standards.

Operating under the tagline "Known Beyond Globe", we've built a reputation for engineering excellence across USA, Qatar, Kuwait, Saudi Arabia, Spain, and throughout India, serving residential, commercial, and critical infrastructure sectors.

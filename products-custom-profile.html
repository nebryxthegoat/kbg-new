Custom Aluminium Profiles

Precision-engineered custom aluminium profiles manufactured to exact specifications. From architectural curtain walls to industrial machinery components—we deliver high-strength, corrosion-resistant extrusions for demanding applications across infrastructure and manufacturing sectors.

Curtain Wall System Profiles

High-performance aluminium extrusions specifically engineered for curtain wall systems. These precision-machined profiles provide structural integrity for glazed facades, featuring thermal breaks, drainage channels, and gasket retention grooves. Designed for commercial high-rise buildings, airports, and institutional projects requiring superior weather resistance and energy efficiency.

Key Features

Thermal Break Technology
High Structural Strength
Precision Machining
Weather Sealed Design
AAMA Certified Systems
Energy Efficient

Applications

  • Commercial high-rise buildings
  • Corporate office towers
  • Airport terminals and transportation hubs
  • Healthcare and institutional facilities
  • Shopping malls and retail complexes
  • Educational and government buildings
Alloy Series 6063-T5, 6061-T6
Wall Thickness 1.5mm to 6.0mm (custom available)
Surface Finish Mill Finish, Anodized, Powder Coated, PVDF
Standards ASTM B221, IS 733, EN 755

Industrial T-Slot Framing Profiles

Modular aluminium T-slot profiles engineered for industrial automation, machine guards, workstations, and assembly line structures. These versatile extrusions feature precision-cut T-grooves on multiple faces, enabling tool-free assembly and reconfiguration. Built to ISO specifications with tight tolerances for industrial manufacturing environments.

Key Features

Modular Design System
Tool-Free Assembly
High Load Capacity
Corrosion Resistant
Precision Tolerances
Reusable & Reconfigurable

Applications

  • Machine guards and safety enclosures
  • Automated assembly line frameworks
  • Industrial workstations and benches
  • Material handling equipment
  • Conveyor support structures
  • Clean room partitions
  • Robotic automation cells
Profile Sizes 20x20, 30x30, 40x40, 45x45, 60x60, 80x80mm
Slot Width 6mm, 8mm, 10mm T-slots

Heavy-Duty Structural Beam Profiles

Custom extruded aluminium I-beams, H-beams, and channel sections designed for structural applications. These high-strength profiles offer superior strength-to-weight ratios compared to steel, with exceptional corrosion resistance for outdoor infrastructure. Manufactured to stringent tolerances for critical load-bearing applications in bridges, canopies, and industrial structures.
